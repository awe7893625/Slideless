S01 · Investment Thesis

SCQA — 為什麼市場可能低估？

S · Situation（現況）

Rocket Lab USA is the world's second-most-launched orbital rocket company (after SpaceX), operating the Electron small-lift rocket (17 successful launches in 2025 totaling $87M launch revenue) and growing a Space Systems segment (satellites, spacecraft components). Q1 2026 revenue was $200.3M, up 67% YoY, with Space Systems contributing $165.4M (83% of revenue) driven by constellation satellite contract delivery. The company's $2.2B backlog (as of May 2026) covers approximately 2.8 years of current run-rate revenue.

C · Complication（問題）

Rocket Lab is not yet profitable: Q1 2026 GAAP net loss was $45M, with non-GAAP operating loss of $12M. The Neutron medium-lift rocket (targeting 13-ton LEO payload) is in development for a first launch target of 2026, requiring significant capital ($100M+ estimated remaining development cost). The company faces direct competition from SpaceX Falcon 9 for Neutron's target market and from Firefly/ABL for Electron's small-sat market.

Q · Question（關鍵問題）

Can Rocket Lab's Space Systems scale and Neutron commercialization justify a US$10.8B market cap on $800M annual revenue run-rate, before achieving GAAP profitability?

A · Answer（我們的答案）

The thesis depends on Neutron: if Neutron achieves first commercial flight by H2 2026 and wins 5+ contract launches by 2027, RKLB's valuation is defensible on a EV/Revenue basis (13x NTM vs SpaceX implied 40x+ on secondary market). Space Systems $2.2B backlog provides visibility. Near-term downside risk: Neutron delay beyond Q4 2026 could trigger 20-30% multiple compression. Base 12-month price target: US$26.

S07 · 供應鏈位置圖

供應鏈位置圖

供應鏈位置圖 — 上游 → Rocket Lab 火箭實驗室 → 下游客戶 → 終端市場

Rocket Lab 火箭實驗室 在 商業航太 / 小型衛星 / 太空製造 供應鏈中的定位：從上游材料/製造合作到下游核心客戶。客戶切換成本高（技術認證週期 12-24 個月）是主要護城河。

供應鏈護城河分析

Rocket Lab 火箭實驗室 在供應鏈中的核心優勢在於： (1) 客戶切換成本：技術認證週期 12-24 個月，客戶一旦導入難以更換； (2) 上游材料控制：與關鍵供應商長期框架協議，確保供應穩定性； (3) 垂直整合程度：核心技術自研，降低對單一外部供應商依賴。 主要供應鏈風險：集中於特定地理區域（台灣 / 美國 / 日本）的製造地政治風險，以及少數上游關鍵材料的供應集中度。

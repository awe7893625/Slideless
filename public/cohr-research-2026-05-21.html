S01 · Investment Thesis

SCQA — 為什麼市場可能低估？

S · Situation（現況）

Coherent Corp. is a diversified photonics and compound semiconductor company formed from the 2022 merger of II-VI and legacy Coherent. The combined entity serves hyperscaler datacenters, telecom networks, and industrial laser markets. Revenue for Q3 FY2026 (period ended March 31, 2026) was $1,805.6M, up 26% year-over-year, with datacom transceiver volumes surging on 800G AI-infrastructure buildout. Networking segment revenue reached $1,118.5M (62% of total), Materials $379.2M (21%), Lasers $307.9M (17%). Non-GAAP gross margin improved from 33.5% in Q3 FY2025 to 37.6% in Q3 FY2026, representing 410 basis points of expansion.

C · Complication（問題）

Despite top-line acceleration, Coherent carries $3.2B in net debt from the merger, GAAP net income remains in loss territory ($-39.5M in Q3 FY2026), and its SiC power device business faces a near-term inventory correction in EV. The company must simultaneously deleverage, ramp 1.6T transceivers, and integrate legacy operations — a three-front execution challenge. Total shares outstanding: 133.6M. Interest expense: approximately $270M annualized at ~6.3% average rate on $4.3B gross debt. Net GAAP loss of $39.5M in Q3 FY2026 vs loss of $188.4M in Q3 FY2025, representing 79% improvement YoY despite ongoing amortization of $2.1B in intangibles.

Q · Question（關鍵問題）

Can Coherent monetize its datacom leadership fast enough to offset SiC headwinds and reduce leverage before 2027, and is the current valuation pricing this transition fairly?

A · Answer（我們的答案）

Yes — with caveats. Datacom transceiver revenue is ramping faster than consensus modeled (800G volumes tracking 40%+ above street consensus estimates per channel checks). The SiC correction is a 2H FY2026 drag but auto OEM design wins are intact. Base case 12-month price target: US$115. Risk-adjusted thesis: HOLD with accumulate-on-weakness bias below US$85. Probability-weighted PT: $113 (Bear $68 × 25% + Base $115 × 55% + Bull $155 × 20% = $113). Current price $96.50 implies 17% upside to probability-weighted PT.

S07 · 供應鏈位置圖

供應鏈位置圖

供應鏈位置圖 — 上游 → Coherent 科技 → 下游客戶 → 終端市場

Coherent 科技 在 光通訊 / 工業光學 / 半導體材料 供應鏈中的定位：從上游材料/製造合作到下游核心客戶。客戶切換成本高（技術認證週期 12-24 個月）是主要護城河。

供應鏈護城河分析

Coherent 科技 在供應鏈中的核心優勢在於： (1) 客戶切換成本：技術認證週期 12-24 個月，客戶一旦導入難以更換； (2) 上游材料控制：與關鍵供應商長期框架協議，確保供應穩定性； (3) 垂直整合程度：核心技術自研，降低對單一外部供應商依賴。 主要供應鏈風險：集中於特定地理區域（台灣 / 美國 / 日本）的製造地政治風險，以及少數上游關鍵材料的供應集中度。

S01 · Investment Thesis

SCQA — 為什麼市場可能低估？

S · Situation（現況）

AST SpaceMobile is building the world's first and only space-based cellular broadband network designed to connect standard smartphones directly to satellites — no special hardware required. The company operates using AT&T, Verizon, Rakuten, and 45+ other mobile network operators as distribution partners. Q1 2026 revenue was $14.7M (vs $13.8M in all of FY2024), reflecting early commercial service launch with 5 Block 1 BlueBird satellites and ramp of Block 2 20-satellite constellation. GAAP net loss was $191M in Q1 2026, with $3.5B cash on hand following the $900M equity raise in Q4 2025.

C · Complication（問題）

AST is fundamentally a pre-revenue company in transition: $14.7M quarterly revenue is nascent compared to a projected TAM of $1 trillion in annual mobile revenue. The company needs $3B+ in additional capital to fully deploy the planned 243-satellite constellation (Block 3+). Satellite manufacturing speed is the critical bottleneck: Block 2 (100 satellites) manufacturing target is Q4 2026, with full constellation completing 2028-2029. SpaceX Starlink is exploring direct-to-device capability. Iridium and legacy LEO operators remain competitive alternatives.

Q · Question（關鍵問題）

Can AST SpaceMobile achieve sufficient satellite density and MNO revenue sharing agreements to justify a US$9.5B market cap before cash runs out, and before Starlink captures the direct-to-device market?

A · Answer（我們的答案）

High-risk, high-reward. The technology works (successful 4G LTE calls demonstrated over standard iPhones with AT&T, Verizon, and Rakuten in 2025). The $3.5B cash runway covers 2-3 years of current operations plus Block 2 manufacturing. The 5-year probability-weighted PT is US$48 (bull case) or US$4 (bear case). For near-term 12-month: base case PT US$25, assuming Block 2 on-orbit completion Q4 2026 and first commercial revenue ramp to $60M/quarter by Q4 2026.

S07 · 供應鏈位置圖

供應鏈位置圖

供應鏈位置圖 — 上游 → AST 太空行動 → 下游客戶 → 終端市場

AST 太空行動 在 衛星寬頻 / 太空電信 供應鏈中的定位：從上游材料/製造合作到下游核心客戶。客戶切換成本高（技術認證週期 12-24 個月）是主要護城河。

供應鏈護城河分析

AST 太空行動 在供應鏈中的核心優勢在於： (1) 客戶切換成本：技術認證週期 12-24 個月，客戶一旦導入難以更換； (2) 上游材料控制：與關鍵供應商長期框架協議，確保供應穩定性； (3) 垂直整合程度：核心技術自研，降低對單一外部供應商依賴。 主要供應鏈風險：集中於特定地理區域（台灣 / 美國 / 日本）的製造地政治風險，以及少數上游關鍵材料的供應集中度。

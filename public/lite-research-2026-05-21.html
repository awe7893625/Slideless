S01 · Investment Thesis

SCQA — 為什麼市場可能低估？

S · Situation（現況）

Lumentum Holdings is a pure-play optical components company serving cloud datacenter, telecom, and industrial markets. The company completed its acquisition of Cloud Light (EO) in Q3 FY2024, which transformed its datacenter transceiver capability. Q3 FY2026 revenue (period ended March 29, 2026) was $808.4M, up 134% YoY, with gross margin 44.2% (non-GAAP). Non-GAAP EPS was $2.37 on revenue that was 94% above the prior year quarter ($416.5M). Cloud & Networking segment grew to $541.5M (67% of revenue), led by 800G EML transceivers and coherent DWDM components.

C · Complication（問題）

Lumentum's 3D sensing / consumer laser business (Industrial & Other segment) is declining as Apple iPhone Face ID suppliers face a product cycle plateau. This segment contributed only $128.4M in Q3 FY2026 (16% of revenue). GAAP net income was -$1.8M (near breakeven) due to acquisition amortization ($48M/quarter). Telecom Component segment ($138.5M, 17% of revenue) faces recovery uncertainty as carrier capex remains cautious.

Q · Question（關鍵問題）

Can Lumentum's AI-driven datacenter transceiver momentum sustain the elevated revenue base and justify continued P/E multiple expansion, despite consumer laser headwinds?

A · Answer（我們的答案）

Yes, with high conviction. Cloud & Networking is now 67% of revenue and growing at 80%+ YoY. Management guided Q4 FY2026 revenue $840-880M (midpoint $860M) with non-GAAP gross margin 44.0-46.0% — acceleration. EML transceiver and 1.6T qualification status gives Lumentum a 12-18 month technology lead over InnoLight. Base case 12-month price target: US$105.
